o You need to count your clothes at the time of pickup and verify it in the SMS sent to you after confirmation.

o Washapp may or may not call you before delivery. If you want us to call you before delivering, please inform our Rider during the Pickup.

o Any special instruction has to be provided to our Knight.

o We do not accept any accessory that is not to be processed.

o All accessories which can be detached from the garment will be counted as separate items and charged separately.

o All garments to be counted and handed over to Knight. Similarly, the counting has to be done during delivery. No claims for garment loss will be entertained if any mismatch is reported after making the payment.

o For any loss or damage of garment, the compensation amount shall not be greater than 10 times of cost of processing that garment. This is to standardize the amount for clothes of different brands.

o The rates are subject to change without prior notice.

o Any issue with respected to Quality has to be reported to us within 24 hours of Delivery of garments.

Cancellations Related

For cancelling your scheduled pickup, you need to call us and inform or you can directly do it in the app. Cancellation is not chargeable as long as the Knight has not left our station to pickup your garments. Otherwise, the customer may have to pay 50 Rupees as conveyance charges.
